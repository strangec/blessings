Grab Bag of Blessings is a sporadic e-letter offering political resources, pickup truck propaganda, recipes, poetry, and other assorted digital ephemera. The project was birthed after I received a letter from a friend mentioning, in cursive, a longing for "an archival body that can never be alone." Formatted as a series of virtual care packages, the grab bags offer a gesture towards digital communalism as much as they do my ongoing ruminations on alternative forms intimate human contact in the wake of chronic illness.
